Our Story

Bringing Beauty to Your Doorstep

GlamCart was born out of a simple idea — making premium makeup products accessible and affordable for everyone, especially in cities like Meerut where options are limited.

We believe every person deserves to feel confident and beautiful. Our curated collection features trusted brands like Lakme, Maybelline, MAC, and Colorbar at prices that won't break the bank.

This project is a college demonstration of modern frontend web development, showcasing responsive design, interactive features, and user-friendly shopping experiences using HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript.
